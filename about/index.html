About

This is the webpage of James D. Mitchell. I am a professor of mathematics at the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of St Andrews. I’m currently interested in computational semigroup theory, and topological algebra.

The website accompanying the paper ‘Groups that together with any transformation generate regular semigroups or idempotent generated semigroups’ by J. Araújo, J. D. Mitchell and Csaba Schneider is available here.
